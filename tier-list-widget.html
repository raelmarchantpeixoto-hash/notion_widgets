Skincare tier list

S
Daily essentials
Sunscreen
Protects skin from UV radiation to prevent damage and aging
Moisturizer
Face-specific formulas only
Reduces water loss and maintains skin hydration
Cleanser
Removes dirt, oil, and residues from the skin surface
Anti-aging
Retinols & Retinoids
Tretinoin (Rx), adapalene, retinol
Increase cell turnover and stimulate collagen production
A
Exfoliation
Alpha Hydroxy Acids
Glycolic · lactic · mandelic · citric · malic
AHA
Exfoliate the skin surface to improve texture and tone. Glycolic = strongest, lactic = gentler + hydrating, mandelic = mildest + antibacterial
Salicylic Acid
BHA · betaine salicylate (gentler form)
BHA
Unclogs pores, good for blackheads and oily skin
Multi-purpose actives
Niacinamide
Vitamin B3
Regulates oil, reduces pore appearance, improves skin barrier
Vitamin C
Ascorbic acid · ácido ascórbico
Antioxidant, brightens skin, helps with dark spots
Barrier & hydration
Ceramides
Ceramidas
Repair and strengthen the skin barrier (especially useful with retinoids/acids)
Hyaluronic Acid
HA · ácido hialurônico
Pulls water into the skin → better hydration and plumpness
B
Targeted acne treatments
Azelaic Acid
Ácido azelaico
Reduces acne, redness, and helps with pigmentation
Benzoyl Peroxide
Peróxido de benzoíla
Kills acne-causing bacteria (for active breakouts)
Hydration
Glycerin
Glicerina
Humectant that attracts water into the skin
Panthenol
Vitamin B5 · pantenol
Soothing, helps healing and hydration
Lightweight occlusives
Squalane
Esqualano
Lightweight oil that prevents moisture loss without clogging
Vaseline / Petrolatum
Petroleum jelly · vaselina
Most effective occlusive known; great for slugging and wound healing. Non-comedogenic despite reputation
C
Anti-aging support
Peptides
Peptídeos
Mild support for skin repair and elasticity (not magic, but decent)
Soothing / anti-inflammatory
Centella Asiatica
Cica · gotu kola
Anti-inflammatory, reduces irritation and redness
Colloidal Oatmeal
Aveia coloidal
Very soothing, good for eczema and reactive/sensitive skin
Cleansing
Oil Cleansers
Demaquilante em óleo
Dissolve sunscreen and sebum better than regular cleansers
Hydration / mild exfoliation
Urea
Ureia
Hydrates and mildly exfoliates depending on concentration
Brightening
Alpha-Arbutin
Alfa-arbutina
Gentle brightening ingredient, works well paired with vitamin C
Tranexamic Acid
Ácido tranexâmico
Solid evidence for hyperpigmentation; increasingly popular
Kojic Acid
Ácido kójico
Brightening agent for dark spots; weaker than vitamin C but cheaper
Plant oils
Jojoba Oil
Óleo de jojoba
Mimics skin sebum, helps balance oil and soften skin
Sunflower Oil
Óleo de girassol
Light emollient rich in fatty acids, supports barrier
D
Pore care
Clay Masks
Kaolin · bentonite
Temporarily reduce oil and clean pores
Heavy occlusives
Lanolin
Lanolina
Heavy occlusive that locks in moisture (can clog pores for some)
Cocoa Butter
Manteiga de cacau · cocoa oil
Occlusive emollient that softens skin but can be comedogenic
Mineral Oil
Óleo mineral · vaselina líquida
Effective occlusive, non-comedogenic despite reputation; very cheap
Formulation aids
PEG
Polyethylene glycol
Solvent/emulsifier that helps ingredients mix and penetrate
Retinol alternatives
Bakuchiol
Bakuchiol
Plant-based retinol alternative; much gentler but noticeably weaker results
F
Avoid
Saliva
Contains enzymes; not suitable or beneficial for skincare use
